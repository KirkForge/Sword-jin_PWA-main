# Chapter 017 — The Frontier Camp
## Act 04: Alliance

### Scene: Imperial Supply Camp — Night

The warden's seal opens supply lines. The first imperial garrison flies Jin's banner alongside the imperial standard. Tonight, the Fang tests the alliance.

A night raid. Hundreds of Fang fighters by soldier-count, but only the elites reach the inner camp. The rest die in the outer perimeter.

### Dialogue

**SOLDIER**
> Commander Jin! The Fang's massing at the eastern treeline — hundreds of them!

**JIN**
> Hold the line. The warden's seal means something now — every soldier on these walls fights under our banner.

**MERCHANT**
> The shaman again. Same trick. We learned this lesson three times now.

**JIN**
> The camp stands. The Fang broke against imperial steel and our blade. Tomorrow we march south for real.

### Gameplay
- **Objective**: Defend the camp for 3 waves
- **Difficulty**: Mid-Act 4 with allies (first chapter with imperial soldiers)
- **Berserker stats**: HP 130, spd 90, dmg 32 (enraged at 50% HP — punish wounded enemies)
- **Reward**: 420 XP, 150g, **Rally Cry** (party-wide damage buff, 5s duration, 8s cooldown)

### Design Notes
- Imperial soldiers use the `ally_imperial_soldier` template (new — added to ally system in the architecture, simplified for chapter data)
- The shaman + berserker + assassin trio is the Fang's elite strike team — they all hit Act 3 stats +20%
- Rally Cry is the first "party-wide" skill. Teaches the player to think about party buff timing
- This is the "co-op mechanics" arrival promised by the Act 4 brief

### Narrative Beat
The alliance's first real fight. The imperial soldiers test Jin's leadership. The merchant and Jin now lead *together* — not just a trader following a swordsman, but co-commanders of a banner that armies answer to.
