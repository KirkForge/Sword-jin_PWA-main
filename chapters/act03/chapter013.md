# Chapter 013 — The Shattered Vow
## Act 03: Betrayal

### Scene: The Merchant Road — Storm

Rain hammers the road Jin has walked twice. The merchant wagon is gone — its drivers fled at the first sign of red cloaks. Now the assassins come, in pairs, with poison blades and the same vanish-step the necromancer's wraiths used.

The vow Jin made at the village — to protect the roads, to find who is raising the dead — breaks here. The Crimson Fang doesn't just take the roads. They take *him*, if they can.

### Gameplay

- **Objective**: Survive 3 waves of Crimson Fang elites (assassin + shaman)
- **Difficulty**: Late-Act 3, pre-midpoint boss. The fastest enemy types in the game.
- **Wave 1 (1 assassin + 1 bandit)**: Tutorial for fighting vanishing enemies. Stun them out of vanish with damage.
- **Wave 2 (2 assassins + 1 shaman)**: The aura returns. Kill the shaman — the assassins' damage buff is +50% without it neutralized.
- **Wave 3 (3 assassins)**: Pure assassin. The "shattered vow" — Jin must use what he learned (vanish punishes, shadow step punishes back).
- **Reward**: **Shadow Step skill** — teleport behind target + 10 dmg, 3s cooldown. The skill Jin learned by watching them.

### Design Notes
- This is the **first skill drop of Act 3** — the player has Whirlwind Slash and Battle Cry from Act 2, but no escape tool. Shadow Step doubles as escape and offense.
- Assassins vanish for 1.0s every 5s. Spirit Edge hits them during the vanish cooldown. Spirit Edge + Shadow Step = assassin counter.
- Shaman in wave 2 applies the same aura as the boss chapter — the player should recognize the priority.
- The chapter title refers to Jin's original vow to defend the merchant. The Fang breaks that vow. The reward is a new one: hunt the priest.

### Narrative Beat
Jin has killed the necromancer. The wraith plague is broken. But the Crimson Fang escalates — not with monsters, but with professionals. The priest is still out there. The merchant roads are still choked. The only way forward is to become what the Fang sends against him.

Act 3 ends with ch014 (next chapter), where the priest is named. Act 4 (Alliance) opens once the Fang's leadership is exposed.
