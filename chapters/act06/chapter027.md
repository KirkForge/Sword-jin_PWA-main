# Chapter 027 — The First Memory
## Act 06: Prophecy

### Scene: The First Memory — The Forge Temple

Jin sees the sword's first memory. The empire that forged it is older than anything Jin knows — a kingdom of iron and law, where the gods walked and the dead rose as soldiers. The forge temple where Remembrance was made still stands, in the vision.

The first wielder appears: a king of conquest, who used the sword to forge a world. He was the sword's first master. The sword remembers his wars.

### Dialogue

**JIN**
> The first memory. An empire I do not recognize — older than the throne, older than the Fang. A forge temple where Remembrance was made.

**PRIEST**
> The first wielder. A king who conquered the world. The sword remembers him — it remembers what he did with it.

**FIRST_WIELDER**
> I forged a world. The sword remembers the world I forged. It will remember what you do with it, Sword of Ruin.

**JIN**
> The first memory fades. I am Jin. I am not that king. I will not forge a world with this blade — I will end it.

### Gameplay
- **Objective**: Survive the first memory-trial in 3 waves
- **Difficulty**: Act 6 standard (skeletons return as the ancient empire's guard)
- **Reward**: 900 XP, 380g
- **Boss wave**: The first wielder (wraith with HP 320)

### Design Notes
- Skeletons and skeleton_archers return — a callback to Act 1, but at Act 6 stats
- The first wielder is a single wraith with HP 320 (highest single enemy HP in the game)
- The merchant returns as Jin's anchor to the present
