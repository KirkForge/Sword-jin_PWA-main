# Chapter 029 — The Third Memory
## Act 06: Prophecy

### Scene: The Third Memory — Jin's Future

The third memory is Jin's own. The sword shows him what he will become if he does not choose. A dark reflection of himself — armed, armored, alone, with the same face but with the sword's hunger in his eyes.

The dark Jin has already killed the merchant. The dark Jin has already killed the bandit king. The dark Jin has already broken the alliance, the throne, the world. The dark Jin is what the prophecy makes of those who do not refuse it.

### Dialogue

**JIN**
> The third memory is mine. The sword shows me what I will become — if I do not choose.

**PRIEST**
> The prophecy has a price. Every wielder becomes the sword. The king became conquest. The betrayer became ruin. The next wielder... becomes the sword itself.

**DARK_JIN**
> You will break the world, Jin. Not because you are cruel. Because the sword will make you. The prophecy does not end. The sword does not let go.

**JIN**
> Then the prophecy ends with me. I will not become the sword. I am Jin. I will put Remembrance down.

### Gameplay
- **Objective**: Survive the third memory-trial in 3 waves
- **Difficulty**: Act 6 penultimate (3 enemy types in wave 2, dark Jin boss)
- **Reward**: 1200 XP, 500g
- **Boss wave**: Dark Jin (HP 400, dmg 70, armor 20 — the most dangerous enemy in the game)

### Design Notes
- The `jin_dark_reflection` enemy is a new type — must be added to the bestiary
- This is the chapter where the player *refuses* the prophecy
- The merchant lives through this chapter (the player has to choose to save him)
- 400 HP / 70 dmg is the prophecy's stat pressure — a refusal is the only way out
