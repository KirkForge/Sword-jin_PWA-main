# Chapter 022 — The Inner City
## Act 05: War

### Scene: The First District — Morning

The imperial army breaches the inner city gate at dawn. The first district is a maze of narrow streets, each block fortified with barricades, wraith patrols, and a necromancer at the center.

The general holds the outer perimeter. Jin leads the inner push.

### Dialogue

**JIN**
> The inner city. The dark tower is three districts away. We take them one by one.

**GENERAL**
> The Fang has fortified each district. Necromancers at the heart of every block. We push through, you cut the head.

**KAELEN**
> The wraiths in the second wave — they remember me. Good. So do I.

**JIN**
> The first district falls. Two more to the tower. The Fang's master is watching now — I can feel it.

### Gameplay
- **Objective**: Clear the first inner-city district in 3 waves
- **Difficulty**: Act 5 standard (assassins at 42 dmg, wraiths at 42 dmg)
- **Reward**: 700 XP, 320g

### Design Notes
- The "district" framing means each chapter is a contained fight with a clear visual identity (streets, then blocks, then the master)
- The bandit king gets a personal moment ("the wraiths remember me") — this is the alliance's *internal* arc, not just external
- The general's "we push through, you cut the head" establishes that Jin is the spear of the army, not the shield
