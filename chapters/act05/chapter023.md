# Chapter 023 — The Second District
## Act 05: War

### Scene: The Second District — Midday

The second district is the Fang's pride. Rune-carved streets, fortified buildings, and a necromancer at the heart of every block. The rooftop assassins strike first — three of them, fast, invisible until they're on top of you.

The general loses two soldiers in the alley fighting. The bandit king takes a wraith through the shoulder. The merchant spends half his potions on Jin alone.

### Dialogue

**JIN**
> Rooftop assassins. The Fang's pulling out their best for the second district.

**MERCHANT**
> Three assassins at once. Rally Cry is going to be everything here.

**KAELEN**
> A golem *and* a necromancer. The master is angry we broke the first district.

**JIN**
> Second district falls. One more. The dark tower is in sight now — the master's window glows red.

### Gameplay
- **Objective**: Clear the second district in 3 waves (assassins → wraiths → master)
- **Difficulty**: Act 5 mid-act (assassins at 44 dmg, 3 simultaneous)
- **Reward**: 800 XP, 360g
- **Boss wave**: Necromancer + golem + shaman

### Design Notes
- 3 simultaneous assassins is the new Act 5 ceiling for elite mob spawn
- The golem gets armor 16 (up from 14 in ch021) — every chapter scales slightly
- The merchant's healing is now load-bearing (heal_amount 30) — a hint that party members scale with the player
