# Chapter 021 — The Imperial Army Arrives
## Act 05: War

### Scene: The Breach — Dawn

The imperial army arrives at dawn. A thousand imperial soldiers in steel and crimson, the throne's general at their head. The alliance has held the breach through the night; now they are reinforced.

The Crimson Fang counter-attacks. They will not give up the wall without a real fight.

### Dialogue

**GENERAL**
> Commander Jin. The throne sends a thousand swords. Yours to command — we hold the breach, you lead the strike.

**JIN**
> The Fang counter-attacks at dawn. They know we hold the wall. Rally Cry before the second wave — that's when the golem comes.

**NECROMANCER**
> A thousand swords means a thousand souls. The master will be pleased.

**JIN**
> The breach holds. The imperial army is here. The war has truly begun.

### Gameplay
- **Objective**: Repel the Fang's counter-siege in 3 waves
- **Difficulty**: Act 5 opens with 4 imperial soldiers + bandit king + merchant (full alliance + 1)
- **Golem stats**: HP 240, spd 50, dmg 38, armor 14 (the toughest golem so far)
- **Necromancer in wave 3**: HP 200, casts summons during fight
- **Reward**: 600 XP, 280g

### Design Notes
- The "thousand swords" mentioned in ch020 now materializes on screen as 4 imperial soldiers in this chapter
- The chapter teaches the player that Act 5 fights will be *larger* — more allies, more enemies
- The general's "yours to command" line sets up the chapter where Jin's alliance gets a real chain of command (general, captains, etc.)

### Narrative Beat
The war has begun. The alliance is now an army proper. The Fang's master is in the dark tower at the center of the inner city. The next chapters will push the siege into the inner city.
