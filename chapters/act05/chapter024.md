# Chapter 024 — The Third District
## Act 05: War

### Scene: The Third District — Dusk

The third district is the master's domain. The tower rises at the end of every street, a black column of red stone. The Fang's last elites guard the threshold — golems, wraiths, and a necromancer of terrifying power.

The general is wounded. Three imperial soldiers fall. The bandit king is bloodied but standing. The merchant has used every potion.

Tomorrow, Jin climbs the tower alone.

### Dialogue

**JIN**
> The third district. The tower rises at the end of the street. We go through the Fang's last line.

**GENERAL**
> The master is summoning. The wraiths in the second wave are not the patrol — they're a horde.

**KAELEN**
> Berserkers. The master's last elites. After this — only the tower remains.

**JIN**
> The third district is ours. The tower door is ahead. Tomorrow I climb it alone.

### Gameplay
- **Objective**: Clear the third district in 3 waves (golems → wraith horde → tower threshold)
- **Difficulty**: Act 5 penultimate (HP 280 golems, wraith horde with 180-HP elite)
- **Reward**: 900 XP, 400g
- **Boss wave**: Necromancer + berserker + shaman

### Design Notes
- 4 wraiths in wave 2 (3 + 1 elite) is the "horde" — visually the screen will be full of ghosts
- The berserker returning is a callback to the ch018 bandit king fight — same enemy type, scaled
- The "Jin climbs alone" line is a *promise* for ch025 — the act 5 finale
