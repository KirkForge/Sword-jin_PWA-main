# Chapter 002 — The Merchant's Plea
## Act 01: Awakening

### Scene: Western Field — Morning

After clearing the skeletons from the western field, Jin finds a merchant caravan under attack. The merchant's guard is wounded, and more undead are rising from the burial mounds nearby.

### Dialogue

**MERCHANT**
> Please — my guard took an arrow to the shoulder during the first wave. We have coin. Just get us to the crossroads!

**JIN**
> Keep your coin. The dead don't need it. But stay behind me.

*(Skeletons rise from the ground, 3 of them)*

**MERCHANT** *(mid-combat)*
> They're coming from the north! Watch your flank!

*(After all skeletons are defeated)*

**MERCHANT**
> I owe you my life. Take this — it's not much, but it's sharp.

*Jin receives a Steel Dagger weapon unlock.*

### Gameplay

- **Objective**: Defeat 3 skeletons and escort the merchant to the crossroads
- **New**: Allied NPC (merchant) who follows the player slowly
- **New**: Weapon unlock system demonstration
- **Reward**: Steel Dagger + 60 XP + unlock Chapter 003

### Notes
- Introduces allied NPC following mechanic
- Shows weapon unlock flow in GameState
- Merchant should have basic follow AI (stays within 80px of player)
- Skeleton positions designed to test flanking awareness
