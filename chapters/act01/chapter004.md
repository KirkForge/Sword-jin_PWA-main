# Chapter 004 — The Gate Opens

## Overview
Act 1 finale. Jin reaches the iron gate at the edge of the old fortress. A full skeleton garrison defends it — four foot soldiers, one captain, and an archer on the parapet.

## Design Notes
- Harder than ch003: more enemies, higher stats, archer adds ranged pressure
- Captain has 6 shields (up from 5) and 140 HP
- No merchant ally — Jin fights alone
- Background: dark fortress red-brown
- Victory = "Act 1 Complete" screen, unlocks captain's blade
- No `next_chapter` — end of Act 1

## Enemy Layout
```
Screen 640x360

Archer (500, 60) — top-centre, raining arrows
Skeletons patrol middle-left and middle-right
Captain (580, 140) — front-right, charges player
```

## Narrative Beat
Jin has fought through the rusted blade, helped the merchant, and killed the fortress captain. Now only the gate remains. The road north leads to the capital — and to whoever is raising the dead. Act 2 will introduce new enemy types and the first real boss.
