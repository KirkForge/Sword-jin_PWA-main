# Chapter 001 — The Rusted Blade
## Act 01: Awakening

### Scene: Mountain Village — Dawn

Cold mist rolls through the pines. The village of Hallow Creek hasn't seen a traveler in weeks. 

Jin is sharpening his father's broken sword—a worthless heirloom—when the screams begin.

### Dialogue

**INNKEEPER** *(off-screen)*
> They're in the western field! Skeletons! Dozens of them!

**JIN**
> That rusted thing won't cut butter... but it's all I have.

### Gameplay

- **Objective**: Defeat 4 skeletons to clear the field
- **Controls**: WASD / virtual joystick to move, SPACE / ATK button to swing
- **Reward**: First blood + "Broken Sword" weapon unlocked

### Notes
- Introduces basic movement + attack
- Skeletons have low HP (30), teach player to dodge
- No skills unlocked yet (skill slot locked)
